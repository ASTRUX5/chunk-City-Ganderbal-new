Our Story

The City of Gold

Born in the heart of Ganderbal, Chunk City is where stories are shared over steaming plates of Afghani chicken, crispy KFC-style strips, and hand-rolled momos. We blend Kashmiri warmth with bold global flavours.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily using locally sourced ingredients. Our spacious, well-maintained interior is perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or a quick satisfying bite.
